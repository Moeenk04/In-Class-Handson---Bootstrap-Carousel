Most Beautiful Beaches in the World

Praia da Marinha, Portugal

Praia da Marinha is regarded as one of the most breathtaking beaches in Europe for its orange limestone cliffs and sparkling blue waters.

Placencia Beach, Belize

Placencia Beach is located off the tip of the Placencia Peninsula along the southeastern tip of Belize. Nestled between the Caribbean Sea and ripe rainforests, Placencia has so much more to offer than just sand and the sun.

Navagio Bay, Zakynthos, Greece

Navagio Bay, Zakynthos, Greece touts brilliant blue waters surrounded by towering cliffs. It's reportedly one of the most photographed places in Greece
